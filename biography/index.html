Biography

November 20, 2023

Baoying HUANG Ph.D. candidate, GSMIEEE

Macao Institute of Systems Engineering and Collaborative Laboratory for Intelligent Science and Systems
Faculty of Innovation Engineering
Macau University of Science and Technology (MUST)

Baoying Huang received a B.S. degree in Network Engineering and an M.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology both from the School of Computer Science and Technology, Guangdong University of Technology (GDUT), Guangzhou, China in 2016 and 2019, respectively. She is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in Intelligent Science and System at the Institute of Systems Engineering, Macau University of Science and Technology (MUST). She has published six regular papers in IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics: Systems, IEEE Transactions on Automation Science and Engineering, and IEEE Transactions on Computational Social Systems, respectively. She has served as a reviewer for a number of journals and conferences. Her research interests include Semiconductor Manufacturing Systems, Scheduling and Optimization, Human-Machine Systems, and Computational Social Simulation.
